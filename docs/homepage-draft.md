# MCGR Framework Home Page Draft

## Hero

MCGR is a unified enterprise operating model for multi-cloud governance, reliability, resilience, FinOps, observability, compliance, and executive technology leadership.

It brings together a family of focused repositories, research papers, templates, scorecards, dashboards, and diagrams into one connected ecosystem.

## Why MCGR

Modern enterprises need more than isolated cloud controls.

MCGR helps organizations build a repeatable operating model for:

- governance consistency
- reliability engineering
- disaster recovery readiness
- cloud cost accountability
- observability and operational intelligence
- compliance alignment
- executive decision-making

## What MCGR Covers

### Governance

Establish policy control, architecture standards, and operating discipline across cloud environments.

### Reliability

Define SLOs, error budgets, scorecards, incident response, and reliability improvement practices.

### Resilience

Align disaster recovery, failover planning, recovery validation, and business continuity governance.

### FinOps

Improve cloud cost visibility, rightsizing, unit economics, and executive cost reporting.

### Observability

Use telemetry, dashboards, anomaly detection, and predictive reliability signals to improve operations.

### Compliance

Support audit readiness, control ownership, risk alignment, and regulated-environment governance.

### Executive Governance

Connect maturity, transformation roadmaps, and business-technology alignment to measurable outcomes.
